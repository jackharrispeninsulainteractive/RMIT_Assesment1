Hi, My Names Jack!

I was born and raised in England and migrated to Australia in 2006. Iv got a burning passion for IT and History and I’m continuously drawn to the ideas about how IT influence our society and culture.
Whilst IT has a broad definition I would say that personally I’m fascinated the most by the concept of sub components coming together to create a new interactive device! its unbelievably fascinating to me!

As a balance to this passion and interest I’m simultaneously drawn back to wild! I love hiking and exploring new secluded locations away from the hustle and bustle of modern life and our technology. Whilst this may seem like a paradoxical interest, I think it serves to counter balance my love for IT, it gives me a appreciation and love for nature and the forces that we don’t understand.
